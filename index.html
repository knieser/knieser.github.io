Kenneth J. Nieser

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Health Sciences Research & Development (VA HSR&D) Center for Innovation to Implementation in Palo Alto and at the Stanford-Surgery Policy Improvement Research and Education (S-SPIRE) Center at the Stanford Medicine Department of Surgery.

I received my Ph.D. in Epidemiology with a minor in Statistics from the Department of Population Health Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. In my dissertation research, I developed methods for improving algorithmic fairness with applications in mental health screening and intervention.

Current research interests:

health care inequities, quality measurement, and algorithmic fairness
