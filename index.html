Kenneth J. Nieser

PhD Student in Epidemiology

I am a PhD Student in the Department of Population Health Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison School of Medicine and Public Health.

My current research interests are in social epidemiology, mental health, and algorithmic fairness. I am investigating how models and algorithms used in health sciences research might inadvertently generate and/or amplify health disparities. I aim to develop analytic or computational solutions to such issues and ultimately improve health equity.
