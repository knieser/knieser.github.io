Kenneth J. Nieser

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Innovation to Implementation within the VA Palo Alto Health Care System and at the Stanford-Surgery Policy Improvement Research and Education (S-SPIRE) Center within the Department of Surgery, Stanford School of Medicine.

I received my Ph.D. in Epidemiology with a minor in Statistics from the Department of Population Health Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My research focuses on identifying and addressing disparities in health and health care across sociodemographic groups and on developing new statistical approaches to support these investigations. I am especially interested in making clinical algorithms used in the process of screening, diagnosing, and treating patients more equitable.
