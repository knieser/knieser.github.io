Dissertation research

Unequal representation of population subgroups in a data sample can lead to inequity in how well statistical inferences generalize. To counter this, I am developing computational methods that automatically (1) detect subsets of the sample for whom results might not generalize and (2) examine how effects and associations vary across population subgroups.
